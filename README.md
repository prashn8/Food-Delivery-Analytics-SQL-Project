# Food Delivery Analytics SQL Project

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes restaurant performance across two major food delivery platforms, Swiggy and Zomato, using PostgreSQL.

The objective was to transform raw restaurant and platform data into meaningful business insights by applying advanced SQL techniques and analytical thinking. The project focuses on revenue analysis, customer ratings, delivery performance, market share comparison, cuisine trends, and city-level business opportunities.

---

##  Business Objectives

The project aims to answer real-world business questions such as:

* Which cities generate the highest food delivery revenue?
* Which platform dominates the market share?
* Does faster delivery improve customer ratings?
* Which cuisines are the most profitable?
* Which restaurants perform well across both platforms?
* Which cities provide the best opportunities for food businesses?
* How do commission rates impact restaurant profitability?
